Shi Qiang

Shi Qiang is a character in the Chinese science fiction novel series "The Three-Body Problem", and an important character in the first and second parts. He was the former captain of the anti-terrorism brigade of the Municipal Public Security Bureau and an intelligence officer of the Earth Defense Security Department.

He purposefully met Wang Miao and persuaded him to accept his protection; he risked his life at an ETO gathering and arrested Ye Wenjie; he proposed the Guzheng Operation to intercept information from the Three-Body World.
