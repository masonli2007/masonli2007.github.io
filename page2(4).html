Wang Miao

Wang Miao is a character in Liu Cixin's science fiction novel series "The Three-Body Problem", and the protagonist of the first book "The Past of Earth". He is an applied physics scientist who mainly studies nanomaterials. He provided "nano flying blades" for Operation Guzheng and made a remarkable contribution to the elimination of the human rebel ETO advent organization.

Operation Guzheng used the "nano flying blade" researched by Wang Miao. During the operation, Evans, an important member of the ETO Advent Faction, died and the United Nations obtained important information about the ETO.
