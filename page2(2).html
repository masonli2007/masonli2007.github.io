Luo Ji

Luo Ji was a classmate of Ye Wenjie's daughter Yang Dong in high school. He also has degrees in astronomy and sociology, and was a professor of sociology at Tsinghua University. However, he was irresponsible, opportunistic, and sensational in his research.

Later, due to his conversation with Ye Wenjie in front of Yang Dong's tomb, he was assassinated by the ETO organization and was selected as the fourth wall-facer of the Wall-Facing Project. From then on, he began his career of thinking about the law of the dark forest and fighting against the Three-Body Problem.
