Ye Wenjie

Ye Wenjie, female, was born in June 1947. She holds a master's degree in astrophysics from Tsinghua University. Influenced by her mentor, Ye Wenjie taught herself electronic engineering and computer science.

She is gentle on the outside but strong on the inside. She is a firm idealist. She is deeply influenced by her father's idea of saving the nation through science. She believes that a higher level of science must lead to a higher level of civilization and morality.
